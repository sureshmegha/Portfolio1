Project: Scratch Story
Course: Computer Science Principles
Duration: Around 8 days
Objective: Develop a story or game on Scratch, with an objective and many alternatives. There should be many states in order for the user to experience variety.

For the Scratch project, my partner and I brainstormed many alternatives to a story that would interest the viewers. We finally ended up with the idea of a Finding Nemo copy, where we replicated the parts of the movie, but allowed our viewers to decide the next moves. We incorporated many sections throughout the movie through coding. The idea contained many fun interactive characters aspects of humour, and a clear problem in the storyline.
